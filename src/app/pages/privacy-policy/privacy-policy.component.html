PRIVACY POLICY

Version 1 effective: March 5, 2024.

Welcome to Mitiget Learning Academy (MLA) a member of Mitiget Group. Your privacy and the security of your personal information are important to us. This Privacy Statement outlines how we collect, use, protect, and disclose the information you provide to us through our website and learning platform.

1. Types of Information Collected:

At MLA, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you register for courses or create an account, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and other contact details.
  • Payment Information: If you make purchases on our platform, we collect billing information such as credit card details and billing address.
  • Browsing Data: We may collect information about your device, IP address, browser type, and cookies to enhance your user experience.
  • Course Progress: Information on your course enrollment, progress, and completion may be stored to track learning outcomes.

2. Purpose of Collection:

We collect this information for the following purposes:

  • Providing access to our courses, learning materials, and educational resources.
  • Processing payments for course enrollment and certification.
  • Personalizing your learning experience based on preferences and course history.
  • Sending important updates, announcements, and marketing communications with your consent.

3. How Information is Used:

The information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • Enabling access to course materials, quizzes, and assessments.
  • Communicating important updates, changes to courses, and promotions.
  • Analyzing user behavior and preferences to improve our courses and platform.
  • Ensuring the security and integrity of our platform and user accounts.

4. Data Security Measures:

We take the security of your information seriously. Here are some measures we implement:

  • Encryption techniques to protect data transmission.
  • Secure servers and firewalls to safeguard personal information.
  • Regular security audits and updates to maintain data integrity.
  • Restricted access to personal data within our organization.

5. Third-Party Disclosure:

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent. However, we may share information with trusted partners who assist us in delivering our services, such as payment processors, course instructors, and certification providers.

6. Cookies and Tracking:

MLA uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and track website usage. By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in our Cookie Policy.

7. User Rights:

As a user of MLA, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You have the right to request corrections or updates to your information.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal data from our records.
  • Opt-Out: You have the option to opt-out of marketing communications at any time.

8. Contact Information:

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding your privacy or personal data, please contact us at mla@mitiget.com.ng or 09162644779, 08067191062.

9. Updates to Privacy Statement:

We may update this Privacy Statement from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The latest version will be posted on our website with the date of last update.

10. Legal Basis and Consent:

By using MLA's website and services, you agree to the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Statement. We rely on your consent for processing personal data and will provide options for managing your preferences.

Thank you for trusting Mitiget Learning Academy with your education and personal information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a secure and enriching learning experience.
